Dental Implants Market

Market Size Statistics 2022 - 2030

The global dental implants market is an attractive subset of the global dental supply and equipment market. Growing dental care expenditure, increasing prosperity and awareness of oral health, as well as the aging population, and technological innovations, are the main drivers for the dental implant market. The market is highly dynamic and diverse, with the presence of many global, regional, and local players offering a broad range of implant products and solutions to end-users. The industry’s major end-users include sole practitioners working in relatively small offices to group practices, hospitals, or DSOs ranging in size from a few practitioners to a large number of practitioners who have combined together or otherwise associated their practices individually. The market continues to experience steady growth due to the growing aging population, increased healthcare awareness, and expenditure on dental care. In addition, the dental practice market continues to benefit from the shift of dental care from hospital settings to alternate-care sites, particularly dental clinics.
